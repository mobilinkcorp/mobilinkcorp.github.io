Where the money leaks

Every day, revenue you already carry slips off your books.

Application traffic and international calls move through your network whether you bill them or not. Three leaks account for most of it — and each one is invisible without inspection.

Leak 01 — Messaging

Grey-route A2P

Enterprise messages delivered through aggregators and intermediaries that bypass your billing, while price speculation erodes your margins. You terminate the traffic; someone else collects.

Leak 02 — Voice

OTT & SIMbox bypass

International calls reterminated as on-net VoIP or pushed through SIMbox gateways. Interconnect revenue disappears, call quality drops, and the source is hard to trace without DPI.

Leak 03 — Reach

Unmonetised audience

You hold first-party signal on every subscriber interaction, yet capture none of the advertising value flowing to OTT platforms built on top of your network.

Low-risk rollout

Start by listening. Switch to enforcing when you're ready.

Mobilink deploys in two phases so you quantify the leak before you touch live traffic — and keep control of every rule when you move to enforcement.

Phase 01 — Passive

Tap, observe, measure

A passive tap mirrors traffic with zero impact on live service. Mobilink baselines your messaging and voice, trains on your network's own patterns, and quantifies exactly where revenue is leaking.
